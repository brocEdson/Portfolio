Who am I?

I am Broc Edson. I program for video games. I am currently a student at Bradley University, but I will be graduating in 2022. I have had a lifelong passion for video games and the process of making them. Over the years I have been involved in many game projects.

Let's take a look at a number of them!

Lingering Legacy

My first professional team project, out on Steam! I worked on a lot of the AI for this project, as well as some bugfixing near the end of development. For my first professional project, I'm really happy with what I've done on it!

To play Lingering Legacy, get it on Steam!

Disaster Golf

This is my senior capstone project. Currently it is just a prototype, but I've already gotten a lot out of it about communicating on teams, taking feedback constructively, and of course, many lessons about programming. I programmed many of the hazards for this game.

Game Jams and Personal Projects

I have also released many other games by myself. Many of these are game jams, where I learned a lot about programming with a quick turnaround. Others were simple personal projects that helped me learn coding with Unity quickly.
